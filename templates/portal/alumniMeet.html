1. Payments

  • All bookings made prior to the meeting must be paid in full to guarantee registration. Once payment has been received, an email confirmation and a receipted invoice will be sent. If payment is not made at the time of booking, registration will be provisional.


2. Alterations to Programme - Cancellation/Postponement of Event
  • IIT (BHU) reserves the right to make alterations to the meeting programme, venue and timings.
  • In the unlikely event of the programme being cancelled by IIT (BHU), necessary adjustments etc. Will be carried out.
  • In the event of it being found necessary, for whatever reason, that the meeting is being postponedor the dates being changed, the organisers shall not be liable for any expenditure, damage or loss incurred by the delegate.

3. Speakers
  • Views expressed by speakers are their own. IIT (BHU) cannot accept liability for advice given, or views expressed, by any speaker at the meeting or in any material provided to delegates.

4. Photography & Filming
  • For promotional purposes, there may be a professional photographer and video production taking place during the meeting. Delegates who do not wish to be filmed or recorded should advise the organisers by email to IIT (BHU) prior to the event.

5. Data Protection
  • By submitting registration details, delegates agree to allow IIT (BHU) to contact them regarding their services. Delegates who do not wish to receive such communications please email help.alumnimeet@iitbhu.ac.in. The contact details of registered delegates will be placed on the attendee list which will be passed to sponsoring companies and to all attendees for them to see who is at the meeting for the purpose of networking and meetings. Delegates who do not wish to be included on this list should advise at the time of booking. No personal details of the delegates would be shared with any entity for promotional purposes or any other reason.

6. Websites & Links
  • The meeting and associated IIT (BHU) websites may link to other websites and networking tools provided for the convenience of the users. The contents of these websites are maintained by their owners, for which IIT (BHU) takes no responsibility. Neither can responsibility be taken for contents of any website linking to this website.

7. Insurance
  • It is the responsibility of a delegate to arrange appropriate insurance cover in connection with their attendance at the meeting. IIT (BHU) cannot be held liable for any loss, liability or damage to personal property.

8. Speakers : Terms & Conditions
  • IIT (BHU) may use the speaker’s name and presentation materials for promoting delegate attendance at the event. Presentations and papers including all associated artwork and illustrations will not be returned unless specifically requested by the author. The papers must be objective and completely free of advertising and commercialism.
  • IIT (BHU) may audio and/or video tape the speaker’s session and the recording may be reproduced and sold as part of the overall meeting materials. This allows delegates to purchase audio/video copies of presentations that they may have been unable to attend.
  • IIT (BHU) may reproduce copies of the speaker’s presentation (eg. PowerPoint slides or supporting handouts) on paper and/or electronically and these may be sold as part of the overall hand-out materials during the meeting and after the event. Any acknowledgement regarding Copyright or support should be included at the end of the abstract/presentation, as these will be distributed to the meeting delegates.
  • Speakers who do not wish to give permission for the above terms and conditions, please email to chair.alumnimeet@iitbhu.ac.in before the commencement of the Alumni meet.
